What Is A Google Business Listing ?

This is an example of what you might get if you searched "esports melaka". The three businesses in the image are known as Google Business Listings. Did You Know?

These businesses also appear on Google Maps. So, even drivers passing by your store can view your business!

Why Should You Care ?

People trust search results on the first two pages of Google. This is free-advertising for your business. So, why waste it when you can make use of it to :

  • bring potential customers to YOUR store
  • visit YOUR website.

How I Can Help You ?

I will help you set up and complete the verification process for your business to be listed on Google. If it is already claimed by someone other than you, the actual owner of the business(yes, this is possible), I will proceed with the process of claiming the business from that individual. Once everything is done, I will hand over the credentials to you.
